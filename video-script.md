## Script

---

**[0:00 – 0:15] — The Hook**

*[Show blank screen or title card: "Guide-Pay"]*

> "12 million delivery workers in India.
> Every monsoon, they lose 20 to 30 percent of their income.
> Not because they stopped working.
> Because floods, platform outages, and curfews stopped them.
> And no insurance in the world covers this.
> We built Guide-Pay to change that."

---

**[0:15 – 0:35] — The Problem**

*[Show persona slide or keep on title card]*

> "Ravi Kumar delivers for Zepto in Hyderabad.
> He earns ₹800 a day.
> July 2024 — IMD issues a Red Alert. Flash floods. Zepto suspends ops.
> Ravi loses ₹640 in one day.
> He doesn't file a claim. There's no claim to file.
> Guide-Pay exists for Ravi."

---

**[0:35 – 1:00] — The Product**

*[Open prototype. Show Login → Zone → Premium screens slowly]*

> "Guide-Pay is parametric income insurance.
> Ravi pays ₹44 a week.
> He selects his delivery zone on a map.
> We geo-fence a 5km radius and monitor it 24/7.
> Three binary triggers: IMD flood alerts, platform outages, and government curfews.
> All third-party verified. All objective. No human discretion."

---

**[1:00 – 1:20] — The AI Edge**

*[Show AI Forecast screen, then Worker Risk Score screen]*

> "But here's what makes Guide-Pay different.
> We don't just react to disruptions.
> We predict them.
> Our AI Forecast Engine analyses IMD rainfall data, zone elevation, flood history, and monsoon intensity.
> 24 hours before a flood hits Kondapur — Ravi sees a warning.
> His coverage is automatically extended. He does nothing.
>
> And every worker gets an Insurance Credit Score.
> Low-risk workers pay less. High-risk workers pay more.
> Behaviour-based pricing. Real InsurTech practice."

---

**[1:20 – 1:40] — The Zero-Touch Claim**

*[Show Claim screen, scroll through activity verification and correlation]*

> "When a trigger fires at 2:19 PM —
> Guide-Pay checks Ravi was actually working.
> Last order: 38 minutes before the trigger. Verified.
>
> Then it checks — were other workers affected?
> 28 out of 33 workers in the same zone triggered claims.
> Disruption confirmed. Fraud risk eliminated.
> Fraud score: 0.04. Auto-approved.
>
> [Show payout screen]
>
> ₹600 credited to Ravi's UPI by 4:31 PM.
> 1 hour and 57 minutes after the trigger.
> Ravi filed nothing. Ravi pressed nothing.
> Guide-Pay handled everything."

---

**[1:40 – 2:00] — The Close**

*[Show Admin dashboard and Heat Map briefly]*

> "On the admin side — a live disruption heat map.
> Real-time flood alerts, outage zones, and curfew areas across every covered city.
> AI forecasts for the next 7 days. Predicted exposure. Loss ratio monitoring.
>
> Guide-Pay.
> Built for 12 million workers.
> Powered by AI. Zero friction. Real impact.
>
> Team SentinelX. KL University."
